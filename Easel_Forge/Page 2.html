Lawrence “Designer J” Jefferson

Newsbf.com Media Design Ninja!

Lawrence Jefferson is the newsbf.com media design department lead . After careers in the lumber industry, merchant marines, and retirement from the United States Army he decided to follow his love of all things web. While recovering from injuries he received while serving in Afghanistan he obtained his Associates of Science Degree in Web Publishing. He became a partner with an online news paper publication newsbf.com becoming the web and graphics designer for them making graphics for banner and buttons ads, one-page micro-sites, and doing the digital photography. He quickly found out that this was to be his new passion; the world of multi-media and the web. He is a currently furthering his skills by undertaking a digital design and animation Bachelors program. He loves what he does and enjoys being able to help people establish their presence on the web!
